What Our Commitments

_
INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY Adroit Solutions Limited, we are committed to the provision of quality financial technology support services to our clients and customers, as well as the continual improvement of our ISO 27001:2013 Information Security Management system and satisfying applicable statutory, regulatory and legal requirements. To achieve this, we have set measurable objectives at all relevant functions and levels throughout the organization.

BUSINESS CONTINUITY POLICY

_

Adroit Solutions Limited is committed to providing financial technology support services to Micro, Small, Medium and Large Enterprises operating in key sectors of the Nigerian economy. To ensure continuous operations across the organization and at all times, Adroit Solutions Limited has implemented a Business Continuity Management System (BCMS) in line with the requirements of the ISO 22301:2019 standard. This we demonstrate by:

The setting of policies, objectives and the provision of appropriate resources to establish and develop the BCMS.
