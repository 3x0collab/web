Adroit Solutions Ltd. is a comprehensive web-based construction project management software that provides a thorough set of management functions in a single configurable package.

Designed to support public and private organizations, construction managers, and contractors, Adroit Solutions Ltd. has multiple modules that combine planning, estimating, cost controls, document controls, scheduling, asset management, and workflow into an easy-to-use program. Thus, users have the option to choose one or more of these modules to address any stage of the Plan-Build-Operate life cycle. In fact, many Global Fortune 500 companies and government agencies choose Adroit Solutions Ltd. as their Construction Project Management Software

Furthermore, supporting projects through the entire process, Adroit Solutions Ltd. provides real-time, interactive business intelligence dashboards and reports that are graphic-rich and actionable, comprising all aspects for tracking and managing capital programs, projects, and assets.
