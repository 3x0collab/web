Welcome to Adroit Solutions Limited

Adroit is a software development company headquartered in Nigeria, with office in Nigeria- Adroit Solutions Ltd. It has been successfully operating for almost 10 years and currently employs more than 50 qualified software engineers and managers.

Adroit develops custom software solutions based on a range of platforms and technologies, including Windows, Unix, Oracle, JAVA, BIRT, Linux, .Net, J2EE, J2SE, DROOLS and other ETL Tools. Our enviable reputation and success in providing pragmatic quality solutions for complex IT projects has resulted in high volume of business.

Adroit Solutions LTD

Our Mission

At Adroit Solutions Ltd, our mission is to deliver exceptional, bespoke software solutions that enhance operational efficiency, drive innovation, and facilitate success for our diverse clientele. With nearly a decade of industry expertise and a robust team of over 50 highly qualified engineers and managers, we are dedicated to providing pragmatic, high-quality solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our extensive proficiency spans a wide range of technologies, including Windows, Unix, Oracle, JAVA, BIRT, Linux, .Net, J2EE, J2SE, DROOLS, and various ETL tools.

Endorsed by prestigious accounting bodies such as The Institute of Chartered Accountants in Africa & Wales and The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants, we are committed to supporting our clients in achieving comprehensive Governance, Risk, Compliance (GRC), and Audit integration. Our modern, market-leading software solutions are designed to offer an unbeatable low cost of ownership while ensuring robust data protection and compliance. At Adroit Solutions Ltd, we prioritize client satisfaction, aiming to build long-term partnerships through unparalleled service, innovation, and technical excellence.

Vision
