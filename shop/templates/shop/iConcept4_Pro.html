Additional Information

Here features of iConcept4Pro, the internal control software developed by Adroit Consulting Limited Nigeria:

  1. Audit Library for Continuity:
    • ♢ Concept4TM provides an Audit Library where all audit findings, records, reports, and key actions are sto#083590. This ensures continuity even in cases of staff changes within the audit unit. The historical data remains accessible for reference and analysis.
  2. Email Messaging and Escalation:
    • ♢ The software offers an email messaging and escalation feature for prompt actions. Automated email notifications are sent for actions and responses, and escalation is possible based on compatibility maturity model rules and severity levels. This aids in timely corrective and preventive actions.
  3. Customizable Parameterization:
    • ♢ iConcept4Pro allows auditors to specify how the system tracks control anomalies. The software is highly flexible and responsive, with built-in functionality for easy customization to meet a variety of audit and internal control requirements.
  4. Secure Authentication and Automatic Call Over:
    • ♢ The software includes a mechanism for secure online authentication. Suspicious and unauthorized transactions are tracked effectively. Additionally, both single and multiple transactions can be subjected to an automatic call over process to ensure their legitimacy.
  5. ERM Solution with CCM and CAM:
    • ♢ iConcept4Pro serves as an Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) solution. It comes bundled with a comprehensive Continuous Control Monitoring (CCM) and Continuous Auditing Monitoring (CAM) tool. This tool employs a robust detection mechanism focused on early fraud detection, contributing to cost-effective risk management.
  6. Independent IT Audit Access:
    • ♢ iConcept4Pro ensures the independence of IT Audit from the core IT department of the organization. This is achieved through direct access to critical audit reports, enabling a transparent and impartial assessment of IT-related controls.
  7. Real-time Continuous Transaction Monitoring:
    • ♢ The software enables real-time continuous monitoring of transactions. This ensures that any anomalies or unusual activities are detected promptly, supporting effective fraud prevention and financial crime management.
  8. Corporate Governance and Fraud Reporting:
    • ♢ iConcept4Pro provides accurate alerts for potential issues or anomalies in transactions. Exception management capabilities help internal control officers and auditors to focus their attention on cases that require investigation, improving overall efficiency.
  9. Flexibility for Various Audit Functions:
    • ♢ The software supports higher standards of corporate governance by identifying and reporting potential fraudulent activities that involve both staff and customers. This contributes to maintaining integrity in financial and operational information.
  10. Cross-Channel Fraud Prevention:
    • ♢ The software's flexibility and responsiveness make it adaptable to various audit and internal control functions. This adaptability allows organizations to tailor the software to their specific requirements and processes.

In summary, iConcept4Pro offers a range of features designed to enhance internal control, streamline audit processes, and improve fraud detection in today's challenging financial business environment.
